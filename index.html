How to join

Getting started is easy. Join the Discord, read the event rules, and sign up for the next tournament.

  • Step 1: Click the Discord link and join the HIDEOUT server.
  • Step 2: Check the #events and #announcements channels for sign-up info.
  • Step 3: Enter the weekly event and show up on time for your match.
  • Step 4: Win points, climb the leaderboard, and earn rewards.

New players are welcome. Our moderators keep the community safe and the matches fair.

Event Rules

  • Arrive on time, join the match lobby, and be ready to play.
  • Use legal Standard format decks for webcam cash events.
  • Submit match results immediately in Discord.
  • Respect opponents, moderators, and the event schedule.

Rule violations can lead to disqualification from cash prizes and leaderboard points.
