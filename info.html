HIDEOUT TCG Info

Your guide to Discord events, tournament formats, leaderboards, community rules, and how to get started.

What is HIDEOUT TCG?

HIDEOUT TCG is a Discord community built around weekly Standard PTCG webcam cash events, Pokémon TCG Live tournaments, and a monthly competitive leaderboard. We welcome all trainers and keep competitions fair with active moderation.

Event formats

  • Standard PTCG webcam events: Live webcam matches using Standard format decks with cash prizes and commentary.
  • Pokémon TCG Live: Online ladder play for points, rewards, and leaderboard ranking.
  • Monthly finals: The top leaderboard players compete in a final bracket for bonus prizes.

Leaderboard system

Leaderboard points are awarded for event performance, placements, and sportsmanship. We track points each month and highlight the best players on the leaderboard page.

FAQ

Do I need to pay to participate?

No. Joining the Discord and learning the rules is free. Cash events may require an entry fee depending on the event announcement.

What deck format is used?

We use Standard format for webcam PTCG events. Pokémon TCG Live events follow the platform's standard competitive format.

Where are results posted?

Results and standings are posted in Discord channels like #events, #results, and #leaderboard.

How do I report my match result?

Submit your result in the designated Discord match report channel immediately after your match ends.

How are payouts handled?

Payout details are announced in the event post. Most cash event payouts are handled via agreed payment methods shared privately.
